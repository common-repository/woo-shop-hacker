Connects WooCommerce with Shop Hacker API so you can add 1000s of digital products to your store instantly for free.

== Description ==

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=65bsGl-Q9S8]

Shop Hacker allows you to easily import digital products such as elearning courses, software, and games directly into your ecommerce store.

BENEFITS:

* Shop Hacker allows any online merchant to expand into the world of digital goods.
* Easily Add Products – Import hundreds of digital products in seconds.
* Make More Money – Achieve 30-50%+ profit margins on your digital products.
* Grow Your Customer Email List – Leverage 100s of free digital products in order to build your list.
* Make Your Own Branded Custom Bundles – Mix and match products to make special digital bundles for your fans.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/woo-shop-hacker` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
1. Use the `WooCommerce->Settings->Shop Hacker` screen to configure the plugin.
